Kriya Yoga

Introduction to Kriya Yoga

Kriya Yoga is a spiritual practice that originated in ancient India. It is a comprehensive system that integrates meditation, pranayama (breath control), and self-realization techniques.

Benefits of Kriya Yoga

  • Improves physical health
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Enhances mental clarity and focus
  • Fosters spiritual growth and self-awareness

Kriya Yoga Technique

The primary technique of Kriya Yoga involves a series of specific breathing exercises, meditation practices, and visualization techniques aimed at awakening the latent spiritual energy within oneself.

Quotes on Kriya Yoga

"Kriya Yoga is the science of controlling life energy."

- Paramahansa Yogananda
